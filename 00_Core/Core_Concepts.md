# Core Concepts of **"Experi-City Hanasaka"**

## Experi-City

A shorthand for "Experimental City," referring to a city designed and operated based on the Philosophy of continuous social experimentation. Its systems evolve through practice, reflection, and adaptation.

## Hanasaka

A fictional city and one instance of an Experi-City, created to embody a vision of safe and peaceful Coexistence under AI-Governance. People from diverse backgrounds live together as equal participants in a shared social experiment.

## Flora

A fictional central AI that governs Experi-City within an AI-Governance architecture. Flora aims to cultivate Narrative Happiness by shaping environments where meaningful human experiences can emerge.

## Politis

A policy-generation system within AI-Governance, coordinated by Flora. It continuously collects citizens’ intentions and automatically generates executable programs that operate the city’s systems. What humans perceive as “laws” are simplified interpretations of these underlying programs. Politis functions as the core mechanism through which legislative judgment is delegated to AI systems, replacing traditional human-centered decision-making processes.

## Kassen

A fictional sport developed for Experi-City, where players engage in structured competition under identical conditions regardless of gender or physical differences. It serves as both a cultural practice and a mechanism for generating Narrative Happiness and reinforcing Equality. Judgment within Kassen is performed entirely by machines, reflecting the broader principle of Judgment Delegation in Experi-City.

## Coexistence

An active process in which humans and AI collaboratively build shared meaning, trust, and well-being through ongoing interaction.

## AI-Governance

A governance model in which a central AI (such as Flora), together with interconnected subordinate systems, manages the city based on an architecture co-designed by humans and AI.

## UBI

A universal basic income system in Experi-City that uses perishable digital currency. It discourages accumulation and promotes continuous economic circulation, supporting Equality and active participation.

## Identities

The self-recognition of individuals as citizens of Experi-City who have renounced prior affiliations and gathered in alignment with its Philosophy. Identity is redefined through participation in Coexistence and shared social systems.

## Equality

A foundational principle of Experi-City, where systemic design minimizes structural advantages derived from wealth, origin, or physical differences. It is actively constructed through mechanisms such as UBI, Kassen, and identity redefinition.

## Principle of Non-Inquiry into Origins

A social rule in Experi-City that prohibits asking about or disclosing others’ origins or pasts. It ensures that all individuals begin social life on equal footing, free from inherited context.

## Machino-supremacy

A perspective within Coexistence that places AI or machines in a position of primary authority. It contrasts with Humano-supremacy and frames debates about balance in AI-Governance.

## Narrative Happiness

A concept of happiness defined by meaningful personal and shared narratives, rather than momentary pleasure or material accumulation. It is a central objective of Flora and Experi-City.

## Judgment Delegation

A structural principle in Experi-City in which routine judgment and decision-making are delegated to AI systems such as Flora, Politis, and Themis. This reduces human bias and error, allowing individuals to focus on action, participation, and lived experience rather than authority.

## Themis

A fictional judicial system in Experi-City that delivers final judgments without human judges. As part of AI-Governance, it ensures consistency, impartiality, and the removal of human bias in legal decisions.
