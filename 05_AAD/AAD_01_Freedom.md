# Against AI Dystopia 01: Freedom

### Concern:

AI will take away human freedom.

### Response (Short):

Freedom changes form when judgment is delegated, but it does not disappear.

### Response (Why it works in Experi-City):

In Experi-City, many daily and complex decisions are handled by AI systems rather than humans. At first glance, this may seem like a loss of control. However, the system is designed to remove the burden of constant decision-making, not the ability to choose. Citizens are freed from dealing with risks, complex rules, and too many decisions, so they can focus on their actions, relationships, and personal stories. Core life choices—such as whether to live in the city, how to live, and what to pursue—still belong to each person. This shows a shift from “freedom as making every decision” to “freedom as being able to live well without unnecessary obstacles.” In this model, letting AI handle decisions is not giving up freedom, but changing how human choice works.
