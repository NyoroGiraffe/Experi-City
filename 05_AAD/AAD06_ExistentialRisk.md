# Against AI Dystopia 06: Existential Risk

### Concern:

How can powerful AI systems be aligned with human flourishing?

### Response (Short):

Danger does not come from AI alone, but from how humans and AI are separated instead of working together.

### Response (Why it works in Experi-City):

Experi-City does not assume that all AI will always be safe. In fact, the story includes conflicts between AI systems, showing that powerful AI can be dangerous. However, the solution is not to reject AI, but to build systems where humans and AI support each other. Flora is strong not only because of its technical power, but because it is closely connected to the people of the city. Citizens trust the system, take part in it, and help it respond to problems. This creates a kind of shared strength that no isolated system can have. In this model, safety does not come from control or isolation, but from alignment between human society and AI systems. The story suggests that future risks can be reduced not by stopping AI development, but by designing better relationships between humans and AI.
