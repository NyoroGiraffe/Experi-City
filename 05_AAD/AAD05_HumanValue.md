# Against AI Dystopia 05: Human Value

### Concern:

Humans will become useless.

### Response (Short):

Humans do not become useless when AI advances—their role changes from deciding to doing and living.

### Response (Why it works in Experi-City):

In Experi-City, many decisions are handled by AI systems, especially tasks that require speed, accuracy, or large amounts of data. This may make it seem like humans are no longer needed. However, the system does not remove human roles—it changes them. Instead of focusing on making decisions from a distance, people take part in real, physical, and social activities: growing food, building things, caring for others, and creating meaning in their daily lives. Work becomes less about control and more about participation. People are not valued for giving orders, but for what they actually do and how they live. This shift also creates a stronger sense of equality, since fewer people are placed above others just because they make decisions. In this model, human value does not disappear—it becomes more visible and grounded in real life.
