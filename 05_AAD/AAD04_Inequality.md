# Against AI Dystopia 04: Inequality

### Concern:

AI will increase inequality.

### Response (Short):

Inequality grows when systems reward accumulation; it shrinks when systems are designed for circulation and equal starting conditions.

### Response (Why it works in Experi-City):

Experi-City deals with inequality not only by redistributing wealth, but by designing the system differently from the start. Basic living conditions are supported by UBI, and land is mostly owned by the city and offered at low cost, which limits wealth gained just by owning property. Also, the use of perishable digital money discourages people from saving too much and encourages money to keep moving through the system. Equality is also supported socially: citizens start with new identities, without inherited status. Instead of fixing inequality after it appears, the system reduces the chances for large gaps to grow in the first place. In this model, inequality is not something that must happen, but something that can be reduced by how society is designed.
