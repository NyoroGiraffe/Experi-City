# Against AI Dystopia 02: Democracy

### Concern:

AI governance is undemocratic.

### Response (Short):

Democracy is not removed, but transformed into continuous, system-driven representation.

### Response (Why it works in Experi-City):

In Experi-City, traditional systems like elections and parliaments are replaced by systems like Politis, which continuously gather and understand the needs and opinions of citizens. At first, the lack of voting or debate may seem like a loss of democracy. However, this system tries to reduce common problems such as misinformation, emotional politics, and unequal influence. Instead of taking part only once in a while, people are represented all the time through their actions, data, and expressed preferences. This does not remove human values, but builds them into a system that can respond quickly and adjust over time. In this model, democracy does not disappear—it changes form. Trust comes not only from voting procedures, but from how well the system reflects people’s real lives.
